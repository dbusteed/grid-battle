HOW TO PLAY

  • OBJECTIVE: defeat all enemy armies
  • BASICS:
    • capture zones to get stronger
    • move next to enemies to attack
    • move next to your own armies to heal
    • level up / split your armies
  • CONTROLS:
    • wasd -- move selected army
    • e -- select next army
    • q -- select previous army
    • 1-9 -- select army by ID
    • WASD -- split selected army
  • DETAILS:
    • armies level up by
      • capturing zones
      • attacking enemies
    • army's "attack power" based on level
    • # of armies is limited by the zones you control
      • after 2 armies, army limit is #zones / 4
      • ex. you need 12 zones to make a 3rd army
